Khawaja Moeen is the Director of several NDIS-based successful businesses, Kirra Health Care Pty Ltd, Rising Stars Community Care Services, Evergreen Care Services Pty Ltd, Orchid Health, and Crescent Community Care. These organizations provide help, care, independence, and quality of life for disabled individuals, as well as various NDIS services. Additionally, he serves as the Managing Director of Blue Ribbon Allied Health Services.

He firmly believes that everyone’s journey deserves personalized care and support, which fosters independence and embraces their aspirations. This motivated Moeen to establish the Care Paradigm, a registered NDIS service provider. This work assists individuals in living with dignity, participating in the community, and making their lives independent.

Khawaja Moeen is also well-known for his construction and design projects since he has a diploma in building and construction. As a Director at Burj Home, he helped people build and design their homes with the expertise of a professional and skilled team. He is also the Burj Group's Executive Director, contributing his expertise to the organization and community.
